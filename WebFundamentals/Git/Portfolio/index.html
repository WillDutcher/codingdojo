About Me

After a couple decades serving as a government contractor in the Intelligence Community, I decided to finally pursue a career in Information Technology. I obtained my IT degree in January 2009 but was never able to fully capitalize on that skillset in my former positions. I was able to build small projects in Excel and Access, but I wanted something more. In September 2019, I accepted a Software Developer/Engineer role. The change was rapid and complete. There was a learning curve, but I am happy in my new role and look to learn more as I complete the MERN (MongoDB, ExpressJS, React, Node.js) stack in Coding Dojo's 14-week part time course!
